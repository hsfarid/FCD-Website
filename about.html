About Us

Home / About Us

First Class Doors has been known for high-quality goods and excellent customer service since its founding in 2021. It was first established in 2019 as an Iridak Roofing Systems subsidiary before becoming independent in 2021. The company today has a reputable and well-established name, and our growth over the past few years is proof of our success. Architects, builders, contractors, and the general public all trust us and choose First Class Doors for their door needs.

Our company's motto, Quality First, drives us continually. The quality of our doors and services speaks for itself, so there is no need for further marketing gimmicks or tactics. We adhere to the adage that was coined by Aldo Gucci in 1938, "Quality is remembered long after Price is forgotten."

As a result, we work hard to increase customer awareness of the wide variations in quality and service offered on the market today. We also seek to increase customer awareness that good quality and service are crucial when buying doors.

Warehouse and Showrooms

Our head office is located at Baron Junction, Off the Adenta Dodowa Highway. We have a showroom where customers can view and inspect our products. Also situated at our head office is a warehouse where all our doors are kept in stock. We also have showrooms at Ashaley Botwe (Opposite Total Energies Filling Station) and Dodowa (Beside Olives Group Construction).

We have a loving, welcoming, and vibrant team of employees who are dedicated to attending to your needs and to make sure you are well satisfied with our products and services.
We give you professional advice and help you choose the right door for your project depending on the environmental condition of your building area, direction of your building, orientation of your door, etc. At First Class Doors, we are one big family and we extend our love to all manner of people. We appreciate and cherish all our customers and it is our goal to give you quality goods and services.
