Internal Doors

Home / Products / Internal Doors

Our internal doors are made from compressed wood and PVC (hard plastic). They are available in big and small sizes. With the use of magnetic locks, you can be guranteed a peaceful and noise-free room as they do not bang loudly. Our internal doors have a smooth surface because they are oven baked after painting, hence dust does not settle on them. The locks are made of steel and very durable.
